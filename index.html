Biography

I am a professor at Zhongnan University of Economics and Law. I received my Ph.D. degree in statistics from Wuhan University in 2010 under the supervision of Professor Yanyan Liu. My research interests lie in developing statistical methodologies and their applications, particularly focusing on high-dimensional data analysis, quantile regression and survival analysis.

I am looking for self-motivated postDoc/Ph.D./master candidates with strong background of statistics, machine learning or applied mathematics. I also provide statistical consulting on the interdiscipline and industry. If interested, welcome to drop me an email with your CV or simple problem statement.
