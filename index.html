Biography

I am an Associate Professor at Anhui University. I received my Ph.D. degree in statistics from East China Normal University in 2019 under the supervision of Professor Riquan Zhang. My research focuses on functional/longitudinal/spatial data analysis, quantile regression, change-point analysis, high-dimensional statistical inference and nonparametric statistics.

I am looking for self-motivated PhD/master candidates with strong background of statistics, machine learning or applied mathematics. I also provide statistical consulting on the interdiscipline and industry. If you are interested in working with me, welcome to send me an email.
